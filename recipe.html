Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

By Chef Maria • 35 mins • Serves 4 • Rating: 4.7 ⭐

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 20 min
Total: 35 min
Yield: 4 servings
Nutrition per serving:
520 kcal • 62g carbs • 21g protein • 20g fat

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crisp.
  3. Beat eggs and mix with grated cheese.
  4. Combine all with pasta and season with pepper.
Chef's Tip: For the creamiest sauce, mix the eggs and cheese off the heat and toss quickly with the hot pasta. Use guanciale for a more authentic flavor, and never add cream!
